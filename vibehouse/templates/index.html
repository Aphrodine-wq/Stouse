What you get

Everything from sketch to keys

We didn't build another project tracker. We built the thing that actually gets your house built.

Plans from plain English

Say "I want a 3-bed ranch with a big wraparound porch" and get back floor plans, elevations, structural specs, and a full cost breakdown. No architect meetings required to get started.

Contractors found for you

We match your project to vetted local contractors across every trade — plumbing, electrical, HVAC, framing, roofing, the works. They bid on your job; you pick who you want.

Live build board

Every task, every phase, every contractor — tracked on a Trello board that updates itself. You always know what happened today and what's happening tomorrow.

Morning reports, daily

Every morning you get a clear report: what got done yesterday, what's on deck today, where the budget stands, and if anything needs your attention. No more chasing people for updates.

Catches problems early

Contractor conflict? Budget creep? Schedule slip? The system spots patterns before they become real problems and walks everyone through a structured fix.

Budget you can trust

Real-time spend tracking against your original estimate, broken down by phase. You'll know the moment something goes off track, not three weeks later when the invoice shows up.

Three steps. Seriously.

1

Tell us about your house

Use whatever words feel right. "Modern farmhouse with lots of windows" works just as well as a 20-page brief. The more you share, the more specific the plans — but you can always start loose and refine from there.

2

Review your options

We'll generate three design directions — each with floor plans, structural details, material lists, and a cost estimate. Pick one, tweak it, or mix and match. Then we start finding contractors in your area.

3

Watch it come together

Your build board goes live. Contractors get assigned, inspections get scheduled, and you get daily updates. The whole project runs on autopilot — you just make the big decisions.
